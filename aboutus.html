About Us

My name is Fatima Ghaddar, I am 14 years old and Lebanese,

I created Aktivita to empower women to increase women employees

in their community. In other words I realized how women are

discouraged to work outside of their homes in many communities

and only do chores. In order to solve this issue I decided

to let women empower other women and give them more

opportunities by teaching them in the classes they have chosen.
